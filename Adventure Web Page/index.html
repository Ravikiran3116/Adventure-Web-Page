Adventure Places

The beauty of India is manifold. The views of North India are best enjoyed from above, when the sun rises and turns the snowy mountains kaleidoscopic, scarlet in a pink- and orange-tinged sky.

Dal Lake in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, is renowned for its picturesque beauty. Visitors can enjoy a shikara ride, a traditional wooden boat, and explore the floating gardens, houseboats, and vibrant local markets.

The magnificent trek to Kheerganga starts from a small hamlet called Kasol in Himachal Pradesh. This is the most well-known trekking destination in Himachal which gives a stiff competition to Shimla.
